The next topic I will make for this project is about phenotypic sex and genotypic sex. There are two types of phenotypic sex as we all know: XY chromosome and XX chromosome. XX is for girls, and XY is for boys. Normally, we all start out as girls (XX chromosome), but when an XY chromosome appears, the egg cell or embryo will start to go on the pathway of becoming a boy. However, there is still a chance that the fetus will develop as a girl. Now, moving on to genotypic sex or genotypes: this is the genetic makeup of an individual, determining whether the embryo will be a girl or a boy. It's the final stage of the decision-making process of the cells, deciding which developmental pathway it will take.
